This pages serves as a sample of some of the material I have created for my online courses. The courses themselves were run through Canvas, a learning managment system. The "freebie" videos were produced weekly and chosen based on student voting. The goal was to keep the students thinking ahead about the next week's assignment and add an extra interactive aspect to the course. The exam reviews were designed to help the students study for their exams and show them how answer input would be run

.

Example of Exam Reviews

Please find below solution videos for your Practice Final. There is a video for each problem. You can also find written solutions here: PracticeFinalSolutions.pdf . These serve as a transcript of what was done in the videos.

The actual exam will be similar to the practice exam and it is also recommended that you study your previous homework problems as well, although make sure you are comfortable with the material here first.

If you have not seen it yet, I recently changed the policy on calculators. You will be allowed a scientific Non-Graphing  calculator on your exam. If you do not have one you can use the built in windows 7 calculator on the computers in the university testing centers.  I reserve the right to remove credit from a problem if no work is shown, the calculator is only for simple addition or function values all Calculus must be done by hand.
